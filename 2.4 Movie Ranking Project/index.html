The Best Movies According to Eugene

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Fast and Furious 1-2

I am a fan of sports cars and in this movie i saw some cool ones.
There were also very exciting street races in this movie, too bad that later on the movies were ruined and turned into a action - shooting movies.

Lord of the Rings

I loved every part of this absolute classic.
It was really awesome watching this in the early 2000's as a kid, it included magic, knights, huge armies clashing in great battles, it remains in my top 3 forever.

Drunken Master

Great classic action-comedy movie starring Jackie Chan, one of my favourite action heroes.
Really funny comedy movie which also included some bare knuckle fights and cascades.
